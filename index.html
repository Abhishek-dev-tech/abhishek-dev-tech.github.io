About

I am a game developer and programmer. I love making games and I have 3 years experience in creating and learning games development.

Since I can remember, I've been a huge fan of video games, and I've been fortunate enough to make my passion my profession.


My passion for programming has only gotten stronger throughout the course of my career as I've encountered new difficulties while trying to make fantastic video games that players would enjoy (hopefully as much as I do). Although I'm interested in every part of game development, my main areas of focus are gameplay, visuals, animation, and systems design. Working in new development areas helps me continue to improve my programming skills.
